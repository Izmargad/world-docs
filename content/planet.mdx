description: 'This section describes the Planet'
---

Planet: Sub Pages are created by making a new folder that is the same name as the parent file.

```bash
root
└── content
  ├── index.mdx #homepage "/"
  ├── codeblock.mdx #codeblock "/codeblock"
  └── codeblock
    └── subpage.mdx #subpage under codeblock "/codeblock/subpage"
    └── subpage-2.mdx #subpage under codeblock "/codeblock/subpage-2"
```
